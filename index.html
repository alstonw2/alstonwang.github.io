About me

I'm a product thinker and program manager with five years at Amazon — first in AWS Sales serving the gaming industry, then as a Program Manager at Amazon Business building the systems and processes that keep teams moving.

My work lives at the intersection of people and process: understanding what a team needs, designing how it should work, and making decisions that hold up under pressure. I've collaborated closely with engineers without being one — which means I know how to speak both languages.

Outside of work, I spend time behind a camera and thinking about what we leave behind. That second obsession led me to found Remnant — a platform for cultural preservation and legacy, starting with Chinese heritage.

Remnant

In development · 2025

The world's first AI platform for cultural preservation — starting with Chinese heritage.

Cultures disappear not because people stop caring, but because no one ever built the right tool to capture them. Remnant is an AI-guided platform where elders share their knowledge, families preserve their stories, and nothing gets lost between generations.

I
AI Family Storytelling
Culturally-aware prompts that capture your memories, values, and traditions — in your voice, for your grandchildren.
II
End-of-Life Organization
Burial wishes, ceremony guidance, document storage — so your family isn't scrambling when it matters most.
III
Cultural Preservation Archive
Every story contributed builds a living archive of Chinese cultural knowledge — regional ceremonies, traditions, language, and ritual.
